We offer courses in...

Computer Programming

Unlock the power of coding to bring robots to life! Learn programming fundamentals and apply them to control and automate robots using Python or Scratch. By the end, you'll be ready to create smart robots that solve problems and complete tasks. Perfect for anyone eager to dive into the world of robotics!

Mechanical Design

Dive into the world of mechanical design and bring your robotic ideas to life! Learn the basics of creating robot structures, choosing materials, and designing moving parts. From drafting to 3D modeling, this course will teach you how to build functional and innovative robots. Ideal for those who want to shape the future of robotics through hands-on design!

Electronics

Power up your robotics skills with the basics of electronics! Learn how to wire circuits, integrate sensors, and power your robots with motors and microcontrollers. This course will teach you how to design and troubleshoot the electronic systems that make robots move and sense their environment. Perfect for those looking to electrify their robotic creations!

Human-Computer Interaction

Explore how robots communicate with humans! Learn the principles of human-computer interaction (HCI) and design intuitive interfaces for controlling robots. This course covers touchscreens, voice recognition, and gesture-based controls, helping you create seamless, user-friendly experiences. Perfect for those interested in enhancing robot interactions with people!

About Us

Join Automata, the robotics club where innovation meets automation! Whether you're a beginner or an experienced builder, our club offers hands-on projects, coding challenges, and teamwork opportunities to design and build robots. Explore all aspects of robotics—programming, mechanical design, electronics, and human-computer interaction—while collaborating with like-minded enthusiasts. Together, we'll bring ideas to life and push the boundaries of robotics!
